How to Take Back Control

Phone dependency can feel automatic, but it can be managed.

The first step is awareness.

Start noticing when and why you reach for your phone.

From there, small changes can make a big difference:

  • Turn off non-essential notifications
  • Set time limits on apps
  • Keep your phone away during focused work
  • Replace scrolling with intentional activities

The goal is not to eliminate phone use.

The goal is to take control of it.

Your attention should belong to you — not your phone.
