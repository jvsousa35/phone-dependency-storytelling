Why Phones Are Addictive

Phones are not addictive by accident.

They are designed to keep your attention.

Social media platforms use features like infinite scrolling, notifications, and reward systems to keep users engaged for as long as possible.

Every time you check your phone and see something new, your brain gets a small reward. Over time, this creates a habit loop.

You check your phone not because you need to — but because your brain expects something.

This is how dependency forms.

It is not about a lack of discipline.

It is about design.
